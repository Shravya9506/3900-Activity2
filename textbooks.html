Text books :

Study material for the course work will be made available on canvas once the classes commence. Along with these study materials the following text books should be helpful for the students to get good knowledge about the course.

  • Required Text: Murach’s Python Programming by Michael Urban and Joel Murach. Fresno, CA: Mike Murach and Associates, 2016. ISBN 978-1-890774-97-4. This should be available from Amazon or the UNO BookStore.
  • Recommended Text: Django 2 By Example by Antonio Mele Birmingham, UK: Packt Publishing Ltd. , 2018. ISBN 978-1-78847-248-7 . (This is a great tutorial book that can be found on Amazon and the bookstore as well as https://www.packtpub.com/.
